Open Innovation License (OIL) Terms

Core Principles

  1. Open Access: All medical cures and treatments submitted through the platform and receiving prize money must be made publicly accessible.
  2. Royalty System: Commercial entities that utilize OIL-licensed treatments for profit must contribute 7.5% of related revenue back to the disease-specific research pool.
  3. Attribution: All implementations must properly acknowledge the original researchers and the platform's contribution.
  4. Reinvestment: Royalties flow back into research pools, creating a self-sustaining funding cycle for future breakthroughs.

For Researchers/Cure Developers

  • Upon verification and prize disbursement, your cure becomes OIL-licensed
  • You retain recognition as the discoverer/inventor in all implementations
  • You receive the full prize money associated with the disease's funding pool
  • You may continue to develop and commercialize the cure yourself
  • You gain privileged access to future platform funding

For Commercial Implementers

  • Free to use OIL-licensed cures in products and services
  • Must contribute 7.5% of gross revenue from OIL-licensed treatments back to the platform
  • Must properly attribute original researchers and the platform
  • May improve upon the original treatment (improvements also fall under OIL)

For Non-Commercial Use

  • Free to use, modify, and distribute for non-commercial purposes
  • Must maintain attribution to original researchers
  • No royalties required for non-profit implementation
